The pins are for the Arduino Uno R4 WiFi. If you do not have this specific model, search the documentation for the 3 SPI pins and a ground.

1 - MOSI - 1kohm resistor - Pin 11
2 - 
3 - 
4 - MISO - 1kohm resistor - Pin 12
5 - GND - GND
6 - CLK - 1kohm resistor - Pin 13

MOSI = Master Out Slave In (Controller Out Peripheral In, COPI)
MISO = Master In Slave Out (Controller In Peripheral Out, CIPO)
CLK  = Serial Clock
GND  = Ground
